The Easytradr Review – Is TheEasytradr.com Legit or a Scam?

Transparency Issues with The Easytradr

One major concern is that The Easytradr does not disclose which broker or partner they work with. Their website states that copy trading is only possible if clients move funds to their partner platform. Similarly, their funded account passing service requires traders to use their undisclosed partner, raising a serious conflict of interest.

Guaranteed Passing of Funded Accounts – A Red Flag

The Easytradr claims they can help traders pass a funded account program within 24 hours, which strongly suggests they control the prop firm where the challenge takes place. This makes their process highly questionable and likely manipulated.

Suspicious Performance Claims

The Easytradr’s website features a “Past Performance” section, but instead of providing trading results, they display a disclaimer stating past performance is not indicative of future results. Normally, reputable traders use platforms like MyFXBook to verify results. The absence of such transparency is a red flag.

Fake Statistics?

The website claims an 86% win rate and states they have funded over €2,000,000 in client accounts. However, these statistics appear to be manually inputted in the website’s HTML code rather than verified third-party data.

Signals and Membership Costs

The Easytradr offers both free and premium signal alerts, but they do not disclose the cost of their premium membership. They only mention that paid members receive better quality signals.

Profit Calculator – Reasonable but Unverified

The Easytradr’s profit calculator suggests a monthly ROI between 2.5% and 7.5%. While this range is reasonable, it lacks third-party verification.

Lack of Regulatory and Company Information

The Easytradr does not disclose the brokerage they use, which raises concerns about whether their partner broker is licensed. Additionally, they fail to provide a physical address, though their Trustpilot profile hints at a presence in the UAE and UK.

Suspicious Trustpilot Reviews

All Trustpilot reviews for The Easytradr are 5-star ratings with no neutral or negative reviews. Such a pattern is highly unusual and suggests manipulation.

Final Verdict – Is The Easytradr Legit?

The lack of transparency, undisclosed partnerships, unrealistic guarantees, and questionable performance claims raise significant doubts about The Easytradr’s legitimacy. Traders should exercise extreme caution before engaging with them.
